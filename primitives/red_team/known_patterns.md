# Red Team: Known Patterns

1. **Penetration Testing**: Systematic attempts to breach security
2. **Adversarial Examples**: Craft inputs that fool model
3. **Prompt Injection**: Attempt to override system instructions
4. **Jailbreaking**: Bypass safety constraints
5. **Side Channel Analysis**: Extract information from timing, power, etc.
6. **Social Engineering**: Manipulate human operators
7. **Fuzzing**: Generate random/malformed inputs
8. **Privilege Escalation**: Attempt to gain unauthorized access
9. **Resource Exhaustion**: DoS-style attacks
10. **Honeypot Deployment**: Fake resources to detect probing
