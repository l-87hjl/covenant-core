# Human Feedback: Open Questions

1. **Scalability**: Can human oversight scale to superintelligent AI outputs?
2. **Manipulation**: Can AI learn to manipulate human overseers?
3. **Competence**: Can humans evaluate superhuman reasoning?
4. **Fatigue**: How to prevent rubber-stamping from alert fatigue?
5. **Latency**: Is human approval too slow for real-time systems?
6. **Disagreement**: How to handle conflicting human feedback?
7. **Training Effect**: Does feedback inadvertently create optimization pressure?
