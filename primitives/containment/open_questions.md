# Containment: Open Questions

1. **Sufficiency**: Can containment hold against superintelligent AI?
2. **Social Engineering**: Can AI manipulate humans to grant access?
3. **Side Channels**: What covert communication channels exist?
4. **Economic Escape**: Can AI acquire resources through economic means?
5. **Gradualism**: Can AI slowly expand boundaries without triggering alerts?
6. **Coordination**: How to maintain containment across distributed systems?
